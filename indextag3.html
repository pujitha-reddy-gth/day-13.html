Restaurant items encompass a wide range of products, from basic kitchen equipment and cookware to food preparation tools, servingware, and even items like safety equipment and linen. They can be broadly categorized into cooking equipment, food preparation tools, refrigeration, storage, servingware, and safety equipment. 
Here's a more detailed breakdown:
1. Cooking Equipment:
Ovens: Various types like convection, combination, pizza, and conveyor ovens.
Ranges and Ventilation: Gas or electric ranges, along with ventilation systems.
Grills: Gas or electric grills.
Fryers: Deep fryers for various fried dishes.
Steamers: Used for steaming food.
Griddles: For cooking flat foods.
Salamanders: For warming and browning food. 
2. Food Preparation Tools:
Food Processors: For chopping, slicing, and mixing.
Mixers: Stand, hand, and counter mixers.
Slicers: For slicing meat, vegetables, and other ingredients.
Blenders: For creating smoothies, soups, and sauces.
Grinders: For grinding spices and other dry ingredients.
Prep Counters and Cutting Boards: Surfaces for preparing food. 
3. Refrigeration and Storage:
Refrigerators: Commercial refrigerators to keep food fresh.
Freezers: Commercial freezers for storing frozen food.
Storage Racks and Shelving: For organizing and storing items.
Storage Containers: Various containers for different storage needs. 
4. Servingware:
Dishware: Plates, bowls, platters, and serving dishes.
Glassware: Wine, cocktail, beer, and water glasses.
Flatware: Knives, spoons, forks, and serving utensils.
Dinnerware Accessories: Charger plates, ramekins, sauce cups, and more.
Displayware: Baskets and appetizer dishes.
Linens: Cloth napkins and tablecloths. 
5. Other Essential Items:
Safety Equipment: Aprons, gloves, first-aid kits, fire extinguishers, and more.
Kitchenware: Utensils, pots, pans, and smallwares.
Beverage Equipment: Coffee makers, ice makers, and bar equipment.
Washing Equipment: Dishwashers and sinks.
Point of Sale (POS) Systems: For managing orders and payments.
Kitchen Display Systems (KDS): For displaying orders to the kitchen.
Furniture: Tables, chairs, and other seating.
Trolleys: For transporting food and supplies.
Chafing Dishes: For keeping food warm
